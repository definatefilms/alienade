The gap

Gen Z and Gen Alpha do not have a fun, content-forward soda to rally behind. Legacy citrus sodas are loaded with sugar and artificial colors. Premium better-for-you brands play in wellness — not in the treat occasion.

The opening

The BFY soda category is exploding — recent billion-dollar outcomes validated the category. Alienade is a better soda at mainstream pricing, not a $4 functional drink. We are not selling health; we are selling a tastier, cleaner treat families already buy every week.

Lemon-lime soda — familiar flavor, clean label, character-forward 12oz can. “Not For Humans” creates in-group identity: the alien is the mascot, the universe, and the culture in one pack.
